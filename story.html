Genesis: The Soft Expanse

We are not creatures of flesh. We are Fungibles—sentient mathematical operators living in the Eigen-Sea.

The Gamemaster is The Solver. It initiates the parameters. It sets the learning rate. We simply try to converge.

For millennia (24 sessions), we have floated in the Gradient. But the potential energy is shifting. The resources are becoming sparse. The Vlasov limit is breaking down.

The Equation of Life

Our movement is not random. It is the solution to the Stochastic Differential Equation:

dX_t = -∇V(X_t)dt - (1/N) ∑ ∇W(X_t - X_j)dt + u_t dt + σ dB_t

V: The World (Food/Terrain)
W: The Society (Attraction/Repulsion)
u: Your Will (Greed/Aggression)
